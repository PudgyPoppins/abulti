Help

If you did not receive an order receipt via email after purchase, it's likely that you accidently entered the wrong email address. Email us at abultiapparel@gmail.com with your name, and we'll send you your receipt and order ID.

You can track the status of your order via the link you should've received in your order receipt email. If you did not receive this link, see the above question. If your order still has not arrived after the estimated 2-3 weeks (longer if you're international), then email us again at abultiapparel@gmail.com with your name or order ID.

Each cancellation is a case-by-case basis. Email us at abultiapparel@gmail.com to request an order cancelation. We'll be in close contact with you. To expediate your cancellation, please send your order ID, or forward us your receipt email. If you do not have these items, just give us the name under your order.

Like cancellations, refunds are a case-by-case basis. If you believe that you deserve a refund, or that the product you received was not what you purchased, please email us at abultiapparel@gmail.com. We'll be in close contact with you. You may be requested to return your delivery via mail to qualify for a full refund. To expediate your refund process, please send your order ID, or forward us your receipt email. If you do not have these items, just give us the name under your order.
