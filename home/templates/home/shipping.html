Shipping

Abulti Apparel offers both domestic and international shipping orders. All shipping is free with your purchase.

Domestic (US) Orders

Domestic shipping is done via the US Postal Service and should arrive within 2-3 weeks after purchasing. If you did not receive your order in this time, feel free to email us at abultiapparel@gmail.com.

International Orders

International order shipping time varies from country to country, but is expected to take at least 3 weeks. While not costing more, international orders do take longer to complete, so please be aware of this.
